ALL ABOUT VINDEX

Blockchain technology is a database mechanism that allows transparent sharing of information in a network that can be applied in building business communities. Blockchain databases store data in blocks that are linked together in a chain. Data is chronologically consistent because you cannot delete or change chains without consensus from the network. As a result, you can use blockchain technology to create an immutable or changeable ledger to track orders, payments, accounts, and other transactions.

Because the use of Blockchain Technology is so important, Vindex was born as a cryptocurrency that is transparent and managed by the community to be offered to the public as an investment alternative based on Blockchain technology.

The Vision & Mission of VINDEX (VINDEX) through smart contracts from Polygon Technology is that in the future it is hoped that an ecosystem will be created that can continue to be developed further so that it can become a leading pioneer in building business ecosystems throughout the world.
