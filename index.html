ALL ABOUT BULLDOG

Bulldog (BULL) Coin was born in 2019, which at that time used Masternode+POS Forked technology from PIVX (QUARK) and is a transparent cryptocurrency managed by the community to be offered to the public as an investment alternative based on Blockchain technology through the creation of BulldogNodes, the use of Proof of Stake, and active governance.

Due to many factors that made it difficult for this project to develop, finally the Development Team took the initiative to rebrand again with a new face to continue the Vision & Mission of BULLDOG (BULL) Coin. Through smart contracts from Polygon Technology,

It is hoped that in the future, with the presence of a new name, namely Bulldog Core, hereinafter referred to as BCORE, the ecosystem being developed can go even further so that it can become a leading pioneer in building blockchain ecosystems throughout the world.
